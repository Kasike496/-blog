HOW TO KEEP FIT

Workout Routine

In order to keep fit and healthy, you should first have a weekly workout routine.
You can either workout in early in the morning or late in the evening. This depends on your daily schedule.
As for me, i prefer working out in the evening to refresh my mind after a tiring day. What about you!
Secondly, each day should have a different type of training to strengthen the whole body.

Additionally, rest is important in your workout routine. In a week you should have one or two rest days for muscles to recover and grow.

Example of a 6 day workout routine

  1. Monday: chest and light triceps
  2. Tuesday: back and light biceps
  3. Wednesday: rest day and optional core workout
  4. Thursday: shoulders and heavy triceps
  5. Friday: Leg and abs workout
  6. Saturday: Cardio exercises

Always remember that if you still look good at the end of your workouts, you didn't train hard enough
